Privacy Policy

Transition Buddy  ·  Effective Date: June 15, 2026

Overview

Transition Buddy is built for servicemembers, veterans, and military families navigating one of the most important transitions of their lives. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Information You Provide

When you use Transition Buddy, you may enter information such as:

  • Your military branch, pay grade, and separation or retirement date
  • Your duty station ZIP code (used to look up BAH rates)
  • Career values and transition goals entered during onboarding
  • Network contacts and notes you add manually

This information is stored locally on your device. It is not uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

Information Collected Automatically

Transition Buddy does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting services that collect personal information.

When the app requests data such as pay tables or VA rates, those requests are routed through a Cloudflare-hosted proxy server. This proxy may log standard technical information (such as IP addresses and request timestamps) in accordance with Cloudflare's privacy policy. We do not use this data to identify you and do not retain it beyond standard server log retention periods.

How We Use Your Information

All information you enter is used solely to personalize your experience within the app — generating your transition timeline, financial estimates, PACE plan, and milestone checklist. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Your profile data is stored on your device using standard iOS secure storage. We do not maintain a user account system or store your personal information on our servers. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Transition Buddy is intended for users 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at support@thetransitionsource.com.
